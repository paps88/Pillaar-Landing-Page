The Process

How it works for your business

Apply & get verified
Submit your NAMM credentials, portfolio, and coverage area. Our team reviews and approves your profile within 48 hours.
Receive design briefs
When a family in your area submits a design, you receive a full brief: stone, shape, inscription, cemetery location, and their budget range.
Submit your quote
Send a transparent, itemised quote through the dashboard. Families compare you against up to three other verified masons in your area.
Win work, build reputation
When selected, you work directly with the family to fulfil the order. After installation, the family leaves a verified review that strengthens your profile.
Partner & list your services
Register your funeral home, add your service area, and create your profile. Families in bereavement can discover and contact you directly.
Receive memorial referrals
When families in your area begin memorial design on Pillaar, you appear as a trusted local funeral director to guide and support them.
Coordinate with stonemasons
Optionally connect to verified stonemasons in our network to offer a seamless, end-to-end service to the families you support.
Build trust & retention
Verified reviews and profile visibility help families find and return to your practice. Build long-term community trust through Pillaar.
